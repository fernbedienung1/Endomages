This Document describes the detection and removal of interferences within Video data.\newline
Concrete: The detection of smoke, haze and staining in medical video data, grabbed from laparoscopes. These interferences will then be removed only within the areas that they ocour in. \\
This so called \Gls{surgical smoke} disables a clear sight to operational areas and therefore delays and complicates surgeries. \\
